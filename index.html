Annals of the Seventh Surge

On the seventh surge, when the sky bore sigils of lightning, the Wardens took their vigil: to shield the realms, to speed the caravans of packets, and to keep watch where the wild nets howl.

  • The crawler legion turned at the Lattice. The fields held.
  • The Veil hummed; the Keep sang back; no hinges scraped.
  • Travelers found the Gate of Names true and swift.
